Simple Sorting

Second Chance gives you the opportunity to not only realize what house hold items are recyclable but also what category they fall into.

The app starts with a camera feature that will allow you to scan a household object you are questioning is recylcable. Once the camera recognizes the obeject it will not only consult you on steps you should take before sorting, but it will classify in recycling terms what category it belongs to.

Finding the right recycling ceneter? Second Chance can navigate you to all recycling centers you need.
